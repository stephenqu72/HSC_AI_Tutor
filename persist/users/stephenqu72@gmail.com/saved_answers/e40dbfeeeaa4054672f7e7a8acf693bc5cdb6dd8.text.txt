### Part i
The amplitude $A$ of simple harmonic motion is defined as half the difference between the maximum and minimum values.
$$A = \frac{\text{Maximum} - \text{Minimum}}{2} = \frac{10 - 6}{2} = \frac{4}{2} = 2$$
**Final Answer:** The amplitude is $2\text{ m}$.

The time elapsed is $7$ hours. Thus, $\frac{T}{2} = 7$, which implies $T = 14$.
**Final Answer:** The period of the motion is $14\text{ hours}$.
