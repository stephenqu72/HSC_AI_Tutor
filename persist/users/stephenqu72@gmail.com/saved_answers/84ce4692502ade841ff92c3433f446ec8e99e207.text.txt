To determine the value of $i^{2025}$, we use the cyclic properties of the imaginary unit $i$, where $i = \sqrt{-1}$.

### Worked Solution

The powers of $i$ follow a cycle of four:
1. $i^1 = i$
2. $i^2 = -1$
3. $i^3 = i^2 \cdot i = -1 \cdot i = -i$
4. $i^4 = (i^2)^2 = (-1)^2 = 1$

Because $i^4 = 1$, any power of $i$ can be evaluated by finding the remainder when the exponent is divided by 4. Mathematically, this is expressed as $i^n = i^r$, where $n = 4k + r$ (for integers $k$ and $r$ such that $0 \le r < 4$).

We divide the exponent $2025$ by $4$:
$$2025 \div 4 = 506 \text{ remainder } 1$$

Thus, we can write:
$$2025 = 4 \times 506 + 1$$

Using the laws of exponents:
$$i^{2025} = i^{(4 \times 506) + 1}$$
$$i^{2025} = (i^4)^{506} \cdot i^1$$

Since $i^4 = 1$:
$$i^{2025} = (1)^{506} \cdot i$$
$$i^{2025} = 1 \cdot i$$
$$i^{2025} = i$$
