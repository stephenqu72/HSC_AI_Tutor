To calculate the ratio $|\alpha| : |\beta|$, we will use the given equations to express $\gamma$ in terms of $\alpha$ and $\beta$, then substitute this into the given geometric condition to relate $\alpha$ and $\beta$ directly.

### Worked Solution

We are given:
1. $\frac{\gamma - \alpha}{\beta - \alpha} = 1 - i$
2. $\alpha + \beta + \gamma = 0$

**Step 1: Express $\gamma$ in terms of $\alpha$ and $\beta$**
From the second condition, we can isolate $\gamma$:
$$\gamma = -\alpha - \beta$$

**Step 2: Substitute $\gamma$ into the first condition**
Substitute $\gamma = -\alpha - \beta$ into $\frac{\gamma - \alpha}{\beta - \alpha} = 1 - i$:
$$\frac{(-\alpha - \beta) - \alpha}{\beta - \alpha} = 1 - i$$
$$\frac{-2\alpha - \beta}{\beta - \alpha} = 1 - i$$

**Step 3: Solve for $\beta$ in terms of $\alpha$**
Multiply both sides by $(\beta - \alpha)$:
$$-2\alpha - \beta = (1 - i)(\beta - \alpha)$$
Expand the right-hand side:
$$-2\alpha - \beta = \beta - \alpha - i\beta + i\alpha$$
Group the $\beta$ terms on one side and the $\alpha$ terms on the other:
$$-2\alpha + \alpha - i\alpha = \beta + \beta - i\beta$$
$$-\alpha(1 + i) = \beta(2 - i)$$
$$\beta = -\alpha \left( \frac{1 + i}{2 - i} \right)$$

**Step 4: Calculate the modulus of $\beta$**
Taking the modulus of both sides, and using the property $|z_1 z_2| = |z_1| |z_2|$ and $|\frac{z_1}{z_2}| = \frac{|z_1|}{|z_2|}$:
$$|\beta| = |-\alpha| \cdot \left| \frac{1 + i}{2 - i} \right| = |\alpha| \cdot \frac{|1 + i|}{|2 - i|}$$
Calculate the individual moduli:
$$|1 + i| = \sqrt{1^2 + 1^2} = \sqrt{2}$$
$$|2 - i| = \sqrt{2^2 + (-1)^2} = \sqrt{4 + 1} = \sqrt{5}$$
Substituting these values:
$$|\beta| = |\alpha| \cdot \frac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{5}}$$

**Step 5: Find the ratio $|\alpha| : |\beta|$**
Rearranging the equation $|\beta| = |\alpha| \cdot \sqrt{\frac{2}{5}}$ gives:
$$\frac{|\alpha|}{|\beta|} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2/5}} = \sqrt{\frac{5}{2}} = \frac{\sqrt{10}}{2}$$
Thus, $|\alpha| : |\beta| = \sqrt{5} : \sqrt{2}$ or $\sqrt{10} : 2$.

**Final Answer:**
The ratio $|\alpha| : |\beta|$ is $\sqrt{5} : \sqrt{2}$ (or equivalently $\sqrt{10} : 2$).
